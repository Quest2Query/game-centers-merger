About Us

The Game Centers Merger Board is a collaborative initiative to unite gaming spaces across Livingstone and surrounding areas. Our mission is to strengthen the community, promote youth engagement, and foster growth through shared experiences, events, and mentorship.

Our Events

Phase I: Engaging Schools & Institutions

We begin by partnering with schools and educational institutions within the Livingstone District. This phase features a 3-day open ceremony to launch the Interschools Game Tournaments, promoting youth engagement, digital literacy, and sportsmanship through gaming.

Phase II: Community & Market Outreach

We expand our impact by involving neighborhood game centers, markets, and local betting facilities. This phase introduces online betting tournaments and outreach into households, fostering inclusive participation and competitive excitement across Livingstone and surrounding areas.
